Our 3 guiding principles

As a long-established and respected veterinary practice, we have a strong point of view, rooted in our values and beliefs. We have 3 guiding principles that we work by on a daily basis and this will never change...

1

Good, Honest Advice

Our vets work on a commission free basis. Their advice is always given with you and your pet's best interest at heart.
2

Exceptional staff

We believe that great staff make a great practice. By offering a nurturing learning environment, our employees' skills are consistantly pushed and we always encourage our vets and nurses to gain further qualifications wherever possible.
3

Time for your pet

There is no time pressure, so vets and nurses can devote time and care for your pet. This allows us to listen to owners, research your options, and treat your pet without having to hurry.

How we help you

We know that your pet is a valued member of the family, so we do our very best to offer the care that you'd expect yourself. We do this with honest, good advice offered in plain English, without a hint of confusing jargon. We also have some great technology that helps to keep your pets healthy and happy, which in turn, makes you happy.

Online booking

Making an appointment online is really easy and means that you can set up a consultation anywhere, at any time. Use the online booking section to set up an account. It only takes a moment.

Convenient locations

All of our surgeries are located within the vibrant little Cotswold towns of Highworth, Fairford and Lechlade. We have plenty of parking at the main surgery and are situated just off the main road.

Investing in staff

We're really proud to say that our staff make the Bridge Vet Clinic what it is today and we have many satisfied customers that will tell you exactly that. Our highly qualified team will guide you through the ups and downs of owning a pet and always make sure that you get the best possible advice, welfare for your animal and price.

24hr emergency care

You can rely on us for peace of mind. We run our own out-of-hours care 24/7, which ensures consistent treatment from the moment your pet arrives at the practice to the time that they leave.

Latest technology

We consistently aim to increase our knowledge and list of technology, in order that your pets get the most advanced care available. To view a technology list, please visit sites specific to your needs.
