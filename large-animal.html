Latest technology

We consistently aim to increase our knowledge and list of technology, in order that your furry friend gets the most advanced care available.

  • Portable ultrasound equipment
  • On site laboratory diagnostics
  • Courier collection for external laboratory work

Treatments

Here is a brief view of treatments that we carry out. We aim to keep as much of the work in-house in order that you have case continuity and your pet has a friendly, recognisable face throughout their time with us.

  • Fertility routine visits using manual palpation and ultrasonography
  • Assistance for mastitis control
  • Sample collection to aid diagnostics
  • Herd/flock health plan preparation and annual review
  • As well as the traditional 'fire brigade' service

Convenient locations

Situated in and around the Cotwolds, our vet practices are conveniently located within Lechlade, Fairford and Highworth. Our Lechlade practice has on-site parking, which allows your pet easy access to your car following surgery and also saves you the stress of having to find a parking space.
