How we help you and your pets

From the moment you arrive, our friendly staff will make you and your pet feel at ease. We always aim to keep your waiting time to a minimum and with two consulting rooms, this is almost always possible. We'll guide you through the consultation without jargon and make sure that you are kept in the loop at every stage, ensuring that your pet gets the best treatment for the best possible price.
Over the years myself and my family have had a number of dogs, and have always been very impressed with the professionalism shown by all the staff at the Bridge Veterinary Clinic. Their personal approach with each client makes your experience at the Lechlade clinic as settling as possible. Whether it's a regular vaccination or a more serious matter, the staff will take the time to examine your animal and give them the time they need!
Anthony Clarke - K9 Centre in Lechlade

Latest technology

We consistently aim to increase our knowledge and list of technology, in order that your furry friend gets the most advanced care available.

  • On site Laboratory diagnostics
  • Courier collection for external laboratory work
  • Digital X-ray facilities
  • Ultrasound
  • Endoscope
  • Compressed air powered dental equipment
  • ECG
  • Blood pressure monitor
  • Capnography and pulse oximetry

Treatments

Here is a brief view of treatments that we carry out. We aim to keep as much of the work in-house in order that you have case continuity and your pet has a friendly, recognisable face throughout their time with us.

  • Annual health check and vaccination
  • Microchipping
  • Advice regarding flea treatment and worming
  • Weight clinics
  • Processing insurance claims
  • Routine neutering surgery
  • Cystocentesis
  • In-house urinalysis
  • Fine needle aspirates and biopsies
  • Skin scrapes and allergy testing
  • Orthopaedic surgery
  • Dental procedures
  • Soft tissue surgery
  • Medical conditions
